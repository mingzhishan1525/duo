How to Play DUO

Basic Rules

DUO is a game of skill and timing. The objective is to match colored blocks or pairs (depending on the game mode) as quickly as possible to earn points and advance to higher levels.

Use your mouse or touchscreen to select and match elements. In multiplayer mode, speed and accuracy are key to outperforming your opponent.

Controls

  • Mouse: Click and drag to select and match items
  • Touchscreen: Tap and swipe to make selections
  • Keyboard: Some modes allow use of arrow keys for navigation (optional)

Tips for Success

  • Focus on accuracy rather than speed when starting out
  • Plan your moves ahead when possible
  • Take advantage of power-ups and special items
  • Practice regularly to improve your reaction time
